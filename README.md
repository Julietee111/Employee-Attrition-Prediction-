EMPLOYEE ATTRITION PREDICTION
Employee attrition prediction is the process of using employee data to identify workers who are likely to leave an organization. High employee turnover can affect an organization's productivity, increase recruitment and training costs, and reduce the overall performance of teams. With the help of data science and machine learning, organizations can analyze historical employee data to discover patterns that are linked to attrition. Information such as age, gender, job role, years at the company, monthly income, job satisfaction overtime, work-life balance, and promotion history can be used to understand why employees leave. The first step in this process is Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), where the dataset is examined to identify trends, relationships, and possible factors influencing employee attrition. After understanding the data, a classification model is developed to predict whether an employee is likely to leave the company or stay. The insights gained from the analysis can help management make informed decisions to improve employee satisfaction and reduce turnover.

Problem Statement

Employee turnover is a major challenge for many organizations because replacing experienced employees requires time, money, and resources. High attrition can disrupt business operations, reduce productivity, and increase recruitment and training costs.

Many organizations struggle to identify employees who may be planning to leave before it happens.As a result, there is a need for a data-driven approach that can analyze employee information, identify the factors associated with attrition, and predict employees who are at risk of leaving.

This project aims to perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) to understand the factors influencing employee attrition and build a classification model that predicts whether an employee is likely to leave the organization. The findings will help management develop better employee retention strategies and improve workforce stability.

Objectives of the Study

The main objective of this project is to develop a predictive model that identifies employees who are likely to leave the organization.
The specific objectives are to:

1.Explore the employee dataset using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA). 2.Identify the key factors associated with employee attrition. 3.Clean and prepare the dataset for machine learning. 4.Build a classification model to predict employee attrition. 4.Evaluate the performance of the prediction model using suitable evaluation metrics. 5.Identify the most important variables influencing employee turnover. 6.#Provide recommendations that can help improve employee retention
